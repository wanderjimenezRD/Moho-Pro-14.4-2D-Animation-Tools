## 🎬 Getting Started with Your First Animation

Here is a simple way to start animating after opening Moho Pro 14.4:

1. **Create a New Project:** Click “File” > “New.” Set your canvas size and frame rate.  
2. **Draw a Vector Shape:** Use the draw tools to make basic shapes for your character or object.  
3. **Add Bones:** Click the bone tool and place bones inside your drawing. These act as joints.  
4. **Bind Bones to Shapes:** Connect your shapes to the bones. This lets you move parts easily.  
5. **Animate Movement:** Move bones frame by frame or use keyframes for smoother effects.  
6. **Use Frame-by-Frame:** If you want hand-drawn motion, draw multiple frames manually.  
7. **Play Back Your Work:** Use the timeline controls to watch your animation.  
8. **Save Regularly:** Click “File” > “Save As” often to avoid losing progress.

## ⚙️ Basic Tips and Controls

- **Timeline:** Shows all frames and layers. Click frames to add or remove keyframes.  
- **Layers:** Organize your drawing into layers for easier editing.  
- **Zoom and Pan:** Use the scroll wheel to zoom. Hold space and drag to pan the canvas.  
- **Undo/Redo:** Use Ctrl+Z to undo actions and Ctrl+Y to redo.  
- **Export:** Click “File” > “Export Animation” to save your project as video or image files.  
- **Help:** Access the help menu for tutorials and manuals by pressing F1.
